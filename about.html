About the Archive

Personal work, memory, and process in one editorial space.

This site is built as a space to slow down: to read images, notes, travel stories, and daily fragments with enough room for context.

Practice

I work across photography, film, design, writing, and digital projects. Rather than separating them into different sites or feeds, this archive brings them into the same editorial system.

Some entries are small — a single image and a few lines. Others are longer — trips, series, essays, or working notes. All of them share the same quiet, minimal frame.
